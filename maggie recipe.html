MAGGIE RECIPE




INGREDIENTS REQUIRED


  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Chilli
  • Peas
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spring onion

STEP 1

Firstly wash all the vegetables and chop them finely.

STEP 2

Now at high flame heat the oil.Now put all the vegetables in the oil and stir fry until cooked properly.When the vegetable gets cooked add some quantity of water to the pan and let it boil properly.

STEP 3

When vegetables get cooked completely put the maggie in the boiling water and let it cook properly.After maggie gets cooked turn off the flame.

STEP 4

Pour your noodles to a beautiful bowl and garnish it with some cheese and spring onions.
